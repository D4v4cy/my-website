Blog #042: Phishing & Social Engineering

If You Clicked That Email, You Deserve What's Coming

(Or: How to Steal Secrets Without Even Trying.)

There's a certain art to deception.

No brute force. No noisy exploits. No malware running wild. Just a well-crafted lie, a little human stupidity, and a click.

Phishing is the oldest trick in the book, yet people still fall for it. Every. Single. Day.

And social engineering? That's just phishing with style.
